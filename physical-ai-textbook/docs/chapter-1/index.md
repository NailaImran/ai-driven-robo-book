# Chapter 1: Foundations of Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence

Welcome to the first chapter of our journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics!

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

- **Define Physical AI** and explain how it differs from traditional digital AI systems
- **Understand embodied intelligence** and why physical interaction with the environment matters for AI
- **Identify key hardware components** required for humanoid robotics (sensors, actuators, compute platforms)
- **Design your own lab setup** and make informed decisions about on-premise vs cloud infrastructure

## Prerequisites

Before starting this chapter, you should have:

- Basic Python programming knowledge (variables, loops, functions)
- Elementary mathematics (algebra, basic calculus concepts)
- Familiarity with command-line interfaces
- Curiosity about robotics and AI!

## Estimated Time

**Total Reading Time:** 90-120 minutes
**Hands-On Exercises:** 45-60 minutes
