# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to your comprehensive guide to **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**! This textbook takes you from fundamental concepts to practical implementation, bridging the gap between digital intelligence and embodied systems.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers three major areas:

### Chapter 1: Foundations of Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence
Understand what makes Physical AI different from traditional AI, explore the theory of embodied intelligence, and set up your development environment.

[Start Chapter 1 →](/docs/chapter-1/)

### Chapter 2: ROS 2 & Robot Control Systems
Master the Robot Operating System 2 (ROS 2), learn to model robots with URDF, implement control algorithms, and deploy to physical hardware.

[Start Chapter 2 →](/docs/chapter-2/chapter-2-index)

### Chapter 3: Simulation Environments & Sensor Integration
Explore physics simulation platforms (Gazebo, Unity, Isaac Sim), integrate sensors, and test your robots in virtual environments before real-world deployment.

[Start Chapter 3 →](/docs/chapter-3/chapter-3-index)

## Who This Is For

This textbook is designed for:

- **Students** learning robotics and AI fundamentals
- **Engineers** transitioning to physical AI systems
- **Researchers** exploring embodied intelligence
- **Makers** building humanoid robotics projects

## Prerequisites

- Basic Python programming knowledge
- Elementary mathematics (algebra, basic calculus)
- Familiarity with command-line interfaces
- Curiosity and willingness to learn!

## How to Use This Textbook

1. **Follow sequentially** - Each chapter builds on previous concepts
2. **Try the code examples** - Hands-on practice is essential
3. **Complete the quizzes** - Test your understanding
4. **Experiment** - Modify examples to deepen learning
